Thank you for making the trip to New Hampshire to join us for our wedding! There are plenty of hotels in the Merrimack, Bedford, and Nashua area. We suggest you stay at the Courtyard Marriot in Nashua, as Labelle Winery has a partnership and a shuttle will run between the venue and the hotel.

If you are flying in to the area, the closest airports are Boston [BOS] and Manchester [MHT]. Manchester is a great little airport, and only 15 minutes from where we live.
