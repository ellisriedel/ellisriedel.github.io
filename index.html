Accommodations

Thank you for making the trip to New Hampshire to join us for our wedding! There are plenty of hotels in the Merrimack, Bedford, and Nashua area. We suggest you stay at the Courtyard Marriot in Nashua, as Labelle Winery has a partnership and a shuttle will run between the venue and the hotel.

If you are flying in to the area, the closest airports are Boston [BOS] and Manchester [MHT]. Manchester is a great little airport, and only 15 minutes from both where we live and the Courtyard Marriot.

Courtyard Marriott Nashua

Check in begins at 3pm. The hotel will do its best to accommodate earlier arrivals, but can't make any promises.

There is a free shuttle service to and from the wedding for guests booked at the hotel. The shuttle will leave the hotel promptly at 4:15pm. It will not wait for any stragglers so please be down to the lobby on time.

Please join us the following morning (8/3/2019) for a celebratory breakfast buffet in the Courtyard Marriott event room.
